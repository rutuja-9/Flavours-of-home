Cup Cakes

Delightful mini cakes that are perfect for parties, birthdays, or a sweet snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • Choco chips or sprinkles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a muffin tray with cupcake liners.
  2. Mix all wet ingredients in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, sift flour, baking powder, and baking soda.
  4. Combine dry and wet ingredients. Mix until smooth.
  5. Fill liners 3/4 full. Top with choco chips if desired.
  6. Bake for 15–20 minutes. Cool and serve or frost.

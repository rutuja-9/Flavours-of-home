Dabeli

A spiced potato filling served in a bun, topped with chutneys, peanuts, and pomegranate â€“ a beloved street food from Gujarat!

Ingredients

  • For the filling:
  • 4 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 2-3 tbsp dabeli masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 1 tbsp garlic chutney
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • For assembling:
  • 8 pav buns
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup sev (crispy chickpea noodles)
  • Chopped coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine mashed potatoes, dabeli masala, red chili powder, salt, tamarind chutney, garlic chutney, and lemon juice. Mix well.
  2. Heat a little oil in a pan and toast the pav buns on both sides until slightly crispy.
  3. To assemble: Spread the potato filling generously on the inside of each pav. Add a few pomegranate seeds, peanuts, and sev.
  4. Sprinkle chopped onions, coriander leaves, and a squeeze of lemon juice on top.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious flavors of Dabeli!

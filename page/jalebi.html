Jalebi

Spirals of crispy, golden fried batter soaked in fragrant sugar syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Few saffron strands
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, cornflour, curd, soda. Let it ferment 6-8 hrs.
  2. Prepare sugar syrup with saffron and keep warm.
  3. Pour batter in spiral shapes in hot oil, fry till crisp.
  4. Dip fried jalebi in sugar syrup and serve warm.

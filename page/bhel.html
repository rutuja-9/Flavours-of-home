Bhel

A crunchy and tangy mix of puffed rice, sev, vegetables, and chutneys â€“ a popular Mumbai street snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1/2 cup sev (crispy chickpea noodles)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup boiled potatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup boiled chickpeas (optional)
  • 1-2 tbsp green chutney
  • 1-2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander for garnish
  • Chopped cucumber (optional)
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine puffed rice, sev, chopped onions, tomatoes, boiled potatoes, and chickpeas.
  2. Add green chutney, tamarind chutney, chaat masala, red chili powder, and salt.
  3. Toss everything together until well mixed.
  4. Garnish with chopped coriander, cucumber, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  5. Serve immediately for the best crunch!

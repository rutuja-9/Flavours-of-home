Moong Dal Chilla

A healthy and savory Indian pancake made with ground moong dal (yellow lentils) and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 1/4 cup water (to make a batter)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp hing (asafoetida)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Soak moong dal in water for 4-5 hours. Drain and grind it into a smooth batter using a little water.
  2. Add cumin seeds, turmeric, red chili powder, hing, salt, and chopped coriander to the batter. Mix well.
  3. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle. Grease it lightly with oil.
  4. Pour a ladle of the batter onto the tawa and spread it into a round shape.
  5. Cook the chilla on medium heat until it turns golden brown on both sides. Flip and cook the other side as well.
  6. Repeat for the remaining batter. Serve hot with chutney or yogurt.

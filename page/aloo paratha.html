Aloo Paratha

A popular North Indian stuffed flatbread made with spiced mashed potatoes â€” perfect for lunch or brunch!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2-3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander
  • Ghee or butter for roasting

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with flour, salt, and water. Rest for 20 mins.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with spices, chili, and coriander.
  3. Stuff mixture into dough balls, seal and roll gently.
  4. Cook on tawa until golden brown on both sides using ghee.
  5. Serve hot with curd or pickle.

Pav Bhaji

A spicy, buttery mashed vegetable curry served with soft butter-toasted pav – a Mumbai street food favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets, boiled
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 capsicum, finely chopped
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter + extra for toasting pav
  • 2 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Lemon wedges, chopped onion & coriander for garnish
  • Pav buns

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped capsicum, and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy. Add pav bhaji masala, chili powder, and salt.
  4. Add the mashed vegetables and cook everything together. Mash continuously while cooking.
  5. Add water as needed and simmer for 10–15 minutes. Finish with butter.
  6. Toast pav buns with butter on a tawa until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve hot bhaji with pav, onion, coriander, and lemon.

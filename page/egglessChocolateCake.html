Eggless Chocolate Cake

A soft, moist chocolate cake made without eggs — perfect for all celebrations!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add sugar, water, oil, vinegar, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Cool completely and frost as desired.

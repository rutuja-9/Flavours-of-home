Veg Roll

A spicy, flavorful Indian wrap made with sautéed vegetables rolled in a soft roti — perfect for lunch or snacks!

Ingredients

  • 2 whole wheat rotis
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (capsicum, carrot, cabbage, etc.)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Green chutney & tomato ketchup

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add vegetables, spices, and salt. Cook until soft.
  3. Spread green chutney and ketchup on roti, add the filling.
  4. Roll tightly and serve warm.

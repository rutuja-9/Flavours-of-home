Kulfi

A traditional Indian frozen dessert made with milk, cardamom, and nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour (optional)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Chopped pistachios and almonds

Instructions

  1. Boil and reduce milk till thick. Add sugar and cardamom.
  2. Add nuts and mix well.
  3. Pour into molds and freeze for 6 hours.
  4. Unmold and serve chilled.

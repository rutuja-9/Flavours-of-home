Shevpuri

Crispy puris topped with spiced potatoes, chutneys, and sev â€“ a delicious and crunchy Mumbai street food snack!

Ingredients

  • For the Filling:
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp black salt
  • Salt to taste
  • For the Topping:
  • 20-25 puris
  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Sev (crispy chickpea noodles)
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Chaat masala (optional)

Instructions

  1. To prepare the filling, mash the boiled potatoes and mix them with boiled chickpeas, cumin powder, red chili powder, black salt, and salt.
  2. To assemble, place a puri on a plate, add a spoonful of the potato-chickpea mixture on top of each puri.
  3. Drizzle green chutney and tamarind chutney over the puris.
  4. Top with sev, chopped onions, and chopped coriander leaves.
  5. Sprinkle chaat masala (optional) on top for extra flavor.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the crunchy, flavorful shevpuri!

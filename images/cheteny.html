Coconut Chutney

A creamy and refreshing chutney made with coconut and tempered spices â€“ the perfect side for dosa, idli, and vada.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated fresh coconut
  • 2 tbsp roasted chana dal (daliya)
  • 1 green chili (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 inch ginger piece
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • For tempering:
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 1 dry red chili

Instructions

  1. Grind coconut, roasted chana dal, green chili, ginger, salt, and water to a smooth chutney.
  2. Adjust the thickness with water as needed.
  3. Heat oil in a small pan and add mustard seeds.
  4. Once they splutter, add curry leaves and dry red chili.
  5. Pour this tempering over the chutney and mix well.
  6. Serve fresh with dosa, idli, vada, or uttapam.

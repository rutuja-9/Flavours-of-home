Jeera Rice

Flavored rice with cumin seeds, perfect to pair with dals and curries for a light, aromatic meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee, add cumin and bay leaf.
  3. Add soaked rice and saut√© for 2 mins.
  4. Add water and salt. Cook until rice is fluffy.
  5. Serve hot with dal or curry.

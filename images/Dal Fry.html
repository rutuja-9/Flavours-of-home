Dal Fry

A simple and flavorful yellow dal tempered with ghee and spices â€“ comfort food at its best.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric & cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook dal with turmeric in a pressure cooker until soft.
  2. Heat ghee, add mustard, cumin, onion, ginger-garlic paste, and tomato.
  3. Add cooked dal and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

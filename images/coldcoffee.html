Cold Coffee

A chilled and creamy coffee drink perfect for summers – rich, smooth, and refreshing!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp instant coffee powder
  • 2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1.5 cups chilled milk
  • 4–5 ice cubes
  • 2 scoops vanilla or chocolate ice cream (optional)
  • Chocolate syrup for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve coffee and sugar in warm water.
  2. Pour the mixture into a blender. Add chilled milk, ice cubes, and ice cream (if using).
  3. Blend until frothy and well mixed.
  4. Drizzle chocolate syrup inside the serving glass (optional).
  5. Pour the cold coffee into the glass and serve immediately.

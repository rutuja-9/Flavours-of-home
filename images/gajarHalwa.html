Gajar Halwa

A rich carrot pudding made with grated carrots, milk, ghee, and dry fruits – a winter special delight!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Chopped nuts and raisins
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee and sauté grated carrots for 5 minutes.
  2. Add milk and cook till it reduces and thickens.
  3. Add sugar and stir until absorbed.
  4. Add cardamom, dry fruits, and serve warm.

Kaju Katli

A rich and silky diamond-shaped cashew fudge that melts in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp rose water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Boil sugar and water to form a 1-string syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder and cook into a dough.
  4. Roll on parchment paper and cut into diamonds.

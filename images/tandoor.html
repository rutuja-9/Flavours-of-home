Tandoori Roti

Traditional Indian roti with a smoky flavor made on tandoor or tawa â€” a great accompaniment to curries.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Butter or ghee

Instructions

  1. Knead a soft dough using wheat flour, curd, salt, and baking powder.
  2. Let it rest for 30 mins. Make small balls.
  3. Roll into thick rotis. Cook on a hot tawa or tandoor.
  4. Flip until golden spots appear. Brush with ghee or butter.
  5. Serve with curry or dal.

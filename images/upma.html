Upma

A light and savory South Indian breakfast made with semolina and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • Few curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Dry roast semolina in a pan until light golden. Keep aside.
  2. In another pan, heat oil. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies.
  3. Add onions and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Pour in water and salt. Bring to a boil.
  5. Slowly add semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook until water is absorbed. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

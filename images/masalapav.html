Masala Pav

Soft pav buns toasted with a spicy vegetable masala – a popular Mumbai street food that is both filling and flavorful!

Ingredients

  • 4 pav buns
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan, add chopped onions, bell peppers, and green chilies. Sauté until soft.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, pav bhaji masala, and garam masala. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add mashed potatoes and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Toast pav buns with butter on a tawa (griddle) until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Fill the pav buns with the prepared masala mixture, garnish with chopped coriander, and serve with lemon wedges.

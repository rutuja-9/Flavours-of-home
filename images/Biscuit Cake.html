Biscuit Cake

A no-bake cake made with crushed biscuits, cocoa, and a touch of sweetness — super easy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of digestive or Parle-G biscuits
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • Choco chips or nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Grind the biscuits into a fine powder.
  2. Add cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder. Mix well.
  3. Slowly add milk and make a smooth batter.
  4. Add melted butter and choco chips if using.
  5. Pour into a greased pan and microwave for 4–5 minutes or bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.
  6. Cool, cut into slices, and enjoy!

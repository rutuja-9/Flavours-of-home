Jawar Roti

A gluten-free, healthy Indian flatbread made with jowar flour â€” rich in fiber and perfect for a wholesome lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jowar (sorghum) flour
  • Hot water as needed
  • Pinch of salt
  • Dry flour for dusting

Instructions

  1. Boil water and mix with jowar flour and salt. Let it cool slightly.
  2. Knead into a smooth dough while still warm.
  3. Make small balls and gently pat into a round roti using hands or rolling pin.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa until both sides are cooked well, optionally puffing on direct flame.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or curry.

Gobi 65

Spicy and crispy South Indian-style cauliflower snack, perfect for parties!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 tbsp rice flour + cornflour
  • Red chili powder, salt, ginger-garlic paste
  • Curry leaves, lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Parboil gobi, mix with spices and flours.
  2. Deep fry till golden brown.
  3. Garnish with fried curry leaves and lemon juice.
  4. Serve hot with chutney.

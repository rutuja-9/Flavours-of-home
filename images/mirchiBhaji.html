Mirchi Bhaji

A delicious fried snack made by stuffing green chilies with spiced gram flour and deep-frying them.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 large green chilies
  • 1/2 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (to make a thick batter)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Make a small slit in each green chili and remove the seeds (optional). Stuff the chilies with a mixture of besan, red chili powder, turmeric, ajwain, and salt.
  2. Prepare a thick batter with besan and a little water.
  3. Dip the stuffed chilies into the batter, ensuring they are coated well.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry the stuffed chilies until golden and crispy.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or tamarind sauce.

Kalakand

A soft and grainy milk-based dessert with a rich taste of cardamom and nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup paneer (crumbled)
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds

Instructions

  1. Mix paneer and condensed milk in a pan.
  2. Cook on low flame till thick and leaves sides.
  3. Add cardamom and mix well.
  4. Spread on a greased tray and garnish with nuts.
  5. Cool and cut into pieces.

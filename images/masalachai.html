Masala Chai

Aromatic Indian spiced tea made with a blend of herbs, spices, milk, and tea leaves – comforting and refreshing!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tsp black tea leaves
  • 2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 small piece of ginger (crushed)
  • 2–3 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves (optional)
  • 1 black peppercorn (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, boil water with crushed ginger, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and peppercorn.
  2. Let it simmer for 3–4 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add tea leaves and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in milk and sugar. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 2 more minutes.
  5. Strain the tea into cups and serve hot.

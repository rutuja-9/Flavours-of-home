Samosa

Golden, crispy pastry filled with spiced potato mixture – a classic Indian snack loved by all!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for kneading)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • For filling:
  • 3-4 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas (boiled)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (optional)
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water and knead into a firm dough. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin, mustard seeds, and sauté ginger-chili paste.
  3. Add potatoes, peas, and all spices. Mix well and cook for 3–4 minutes. Cool the mixture.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each into an oval and cut in half.
  5. Form a cone with each half, fill with stuffing, and seal the edges with water.
  6. Heat oil and deep fry the samosas on medium heat until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve hot with chutneys or tea.

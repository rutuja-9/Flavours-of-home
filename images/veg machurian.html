Veg Manchurian

Indo-Chinese delight with fried veggie balls tossed in spicy tangy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups finely chopped veggies (cabbage, carrot)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce
  • Garlic, onion, capsicum
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix veggies, flours, and shape into balls. Deep fry them.
  2. Saut√© garlic, onion, capsicum. Add sauces and water.
  3. Add balls, toss well and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with noodles or rice.

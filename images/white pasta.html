White Sauce Pasta

Creamy and cheesy white sauce pasta loaded with veggies and Italian flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled pasta
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup cheese
  • Boiled veggies (carrot, corn, capsicum)
  • Salt, pepper, oregano

Instructions

  1. Heat butter, add flour and cook for a minute.
  2. Add milk gradually, stir to avoid lumps.
  3. Add cheese and seasonings. Mix in boiled pasta and veggies.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot.

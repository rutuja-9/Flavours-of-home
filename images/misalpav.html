Misal Pav

A spicy Maharashtrian dish made with sprouted beans and served with pav (bread rolls).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sprouted beans (moong, moth, or mixed)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Pav (bread rolls) for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, and garam masala. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Add sprouted beans, salt, and water. Cook until the beans are soft and the mixture becomes thick.
  4. Serve the misal hot with pav, garnished with fresh coriander.

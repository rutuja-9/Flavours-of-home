Sambhar

A tangy and flavorful South Indian lentil stew made with toor dal, tamarind, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 1/2 cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrot, drumstick, beans)
  • 1 tbsp sambhar powder
  • 1 small ball of tamarind (soaked)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt and turmeric to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook toor dal until soft. Boil vegetables separately.
  2. Combine dal, veggies, tamarind water, and sambhar powder.
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Prepare tempering with mustard, red chilies, and curry leaves. Add to sambhar.
  5. Serve hot with rice or idli.

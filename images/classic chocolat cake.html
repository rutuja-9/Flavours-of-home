Classic Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake that never goes out of style — perfect for birthdays and celebrations!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 and 1/2 cups sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour cake pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat well.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool completely and frost as desired.

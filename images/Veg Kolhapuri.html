Veg Kolhapuri

A spicy mixed vegetable curry from Kolhapur, known for its bold flavors and thick gravy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, cauliflower, peas)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Kolhapuri masala or garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric & chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the vegetables and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil, saut√© onion, add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree.
  3. Add spices and cook till oil separates.
  4. Add boiled veggies and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Serve with roti, naan, or rice.

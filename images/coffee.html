Coffee

A strong and energizing hot beverage made with instant coffee, milk, and sugar – simple and classic!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp instant coffee powder
  • 1–2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. In a cup, mix coffee powder and sugar. Add 2 tbsp of hot water.
  2. Whisk or beat the mixture until light and frothy.
  3. Boil the milk in a saucepan.
  4. Pour the hot milk into the cup with the frothy coffee mixture.
  5. Stir gently and serve hot.

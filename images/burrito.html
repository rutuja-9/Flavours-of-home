Burrito

A soft tortilla wrap filled with rice, beans, veggies, and spicy sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup kidney beans
  • Chopped lettuce, onion, tomato
  • Cheese, sour cream, salsa

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortillas. Layer rice, beans, veggies, cheese, and sauces.
  2. Roll tightly into a burrito. Serve warm.

Black Forest Cake

A rich, chocolate sponge layered with whipped cream and cherries â€” a classic dessert delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 chocolate sponge cake (eggless or with egg)
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup cherry syrup
  • 1/2 cup cherries
  • Chocolate shavings for decoration

Instructions

  1. Slice the sponge cake into two or three layers.
  2. Sprinkle cherry syrup on each layer.
  3. Spread whipped cream and cherries between layers.
  4. Cover entire cake with cream, decorate with chocolate shavings and cherries.
  5. Chill before serving.

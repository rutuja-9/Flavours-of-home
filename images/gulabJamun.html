Gulab Jamun

Soft, sweet, and juicy milk-solid dumplings soaked in rose-scented sugar syrup â€“ a classic Indian dessert!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup khoya (mawa)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Milk (as needed to knead)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Few drops of rose water
  • Cardamom pods (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, cardamom, and rose water. Set aside.
  2. Mix khoya, flour, and baking soda. Add milk little by little and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Make small balls with no cracks.
  4. Deep fry on low flame until golden brown.
  5. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 1 hour.
  6. Serve warm or chilled.

Dal Makhani

A rich and creamy lentil dish made with black urad dal and rajma, cooked slowly for deep flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup whole urad dal
  • 2 tbsp rajma (kidney beans)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • Salt & chili powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak dal and rajma overnight. Pressure cook until soft.
  2. Saut√© onion, add ginger-garlic paste, then tomato puree.
  3. Add boiled dal-rajma, butter, and cook for 30 minutes on low heat.
  4. Add cream before serving. Serve with naan or jeera rice.

Fried Rice

A quick and delicious rice dish tossed with veggies and sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/4 cup chopped beans
  • 2 tbsp chopped spring onions
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok or pan. Add garlic and saut√© for a few seconds.
  2. Add all chopped vegetables. Stir-fry on high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add the cooked rice and mix gently to combine all ingredients.
  5. Cook for another 2-3 minutes. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

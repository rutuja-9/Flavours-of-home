Puran Poli

A traditional sweet flatbread stuffed with jaggery and chana dal â€” a Maharashtrian festive delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chana dal
  • 3/4 cup jaggery (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Water as needed
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Cook chana dal till soft. Drain water and mash.
  2. Add jaggery to dal, cook until mixture thickens. Add cardamom.
  3. Knead flour with water and a bit of salt. Rest for 20 minutes.
  4. Divide dough and stuffing. Flatten dough, add stuffing, seal and roll.
  5. Cook both sides on tawa with ghee until golden. Serve hot.

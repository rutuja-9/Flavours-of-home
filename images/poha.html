Poha

A quick and flavorful flattened rice dish, perfect for a light breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups thick poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Few curry leaves
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Rinse poha in a sieve under running water. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  3. Add onions and saut√© till soft. Add turmeric powder.
  4. Mix in the drained poha, salt, and sugar. Stir gently.
  5. Cover and steam for 2-3 minutes. Turn off the flame.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve with lemon wedges.

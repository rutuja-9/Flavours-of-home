Paneer Butter Masala

A rich and creamy tomato-based curry with soft paneer cubes, perfect with naan or roti.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter, saut√© onions, and add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook till oil separates.
  3. Add paneer and cook for 5 minutes. Stir in cream.
  4. Serve hot with naan or jeera rice.

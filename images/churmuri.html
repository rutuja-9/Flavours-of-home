Churmuri

A light and tangy puffed rice snack from Karnataka â€” full of crunch and flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together just before serving to retain the crunch.
  2. Adjust salt, chili, and lemon as per taste.
  3. Serve immediately for best texture and flavor.

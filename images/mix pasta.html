Mix Sauce Pasta

A delicious fusion of red and white sauces tossed with pasta and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled pasta
  • 1/2 cup red pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup white sauce
  • 1/4 cup cheese
  • Boiled vegetables (corn, capsicum, etc.)
  • Salt, oregano, chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Heat both sauces in a pan and combine well.
  2. Add boiled pasta and veggies.
  3. Mix well, season, and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Garnish with cheese and serve hot.

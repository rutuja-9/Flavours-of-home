Kachori

Crispy, flaky pastry stuffed with spicy lentil or potato filling – a popular Indian snack, perfect with chutneys.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup semolina (rava)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for kneading)
  • For filling:
  • 1/2 cup moong dal or chana dal, soaked for 2-3 hours
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, semolina, salt, and oil. Gradually add water and knead into a smooth dough. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. For the filling: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, then the soaked and drained dal. Sauté for a few minutes.
  3. Add spices, salt, and cook until the dal is cooked through. Let it cool.
  4. Divide dough into small balls. Flatten each into a small disc, place some filling in the center, and seal the edges.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the kachoris on medium heat until golden brown and crisp.
  6. Serve hot with tamarind chutney or mint chutney.

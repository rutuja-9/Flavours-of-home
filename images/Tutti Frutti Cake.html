Tutti Frutti Cake

A colorful, eggless sponge cake studded with candied fruits — a tea-time favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup tutti frutti (tossed in flour)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a loaf or round pan.
  2. Mix curd and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Add oil and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Sift flour, baking powder, and soda. Add gradually to wet mix.
  5. Add milk to adjust batter consistency.
  6. Fold in flour-coated tutti frutti.
  7. Bake for 35–40 minutes. Cool and slice.

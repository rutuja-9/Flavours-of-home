Idli

Soft and fluffy steamed rice cakes, a popular and healthy South Indian breakfast dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for soaking and grinding)
  • Oil (for greasing idli moulds)

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal to a fluffy consistency. Grind rice to a slightly coarse batter.
  3. Mix both batters, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours in a warm place.
  4. Grease idli moulds and pour the batter into each mould.
  5. Steam for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool slightly, remove idlis, and serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Lassi

A traditional and refreshing Punjabi drink made with curd, sugar, and cardamom – thick, creamy, and satisfying!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup chilled water or milk
  • 2–3 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnish (optional)
  • Few saffron strands (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk curd until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and chilled water or milk.
  3. Blend or whisk well until frothy.
  4. Pour into a glass, garnish with chopped nuts and saffron.
  5. Serve chilled.

Poori

Golden, puffed Indian bread that's crispy on the outside and soft inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil (for dough)
  • Water as needed (for kneading)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and 1 tbsp oil. Add water gradually and knead into a firm dough.
  2. Cover and rest the dough for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into a small disc (not too thin).
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan. When hot, slide in a rolled poori.
  5. Press gently with a slotted spoon until it puffs. Flip and cook the other side.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with curry or pickle.

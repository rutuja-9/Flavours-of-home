Limbu Pani (Nimbu Pani)

A simple, refreshing, and hydrating Indian lemonade made with lemon juice, sugar, salt, and chilled water.

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black salt (optional)
  • 2 cups chilled water
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Squeeze the juice from the lemons into a bowl.
  2. Add sugar, salt, and black salt. Mix well until sugar dissolves.
  3. Add chilled water and mix again.
  4. Pour into serving glasses with ice cubes.
  5. Garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.

Uttapam

A thick, savory South Indian pancake topped with fresh vegetables â€“ soft inside, crisp outside, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter (fermented)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste (if not in batter)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick or cast-iron tawa and grease lightly with oil.
  2. Pour a ladle of dosa batter and spread it slightly to form a thick pancake.
  3. Sprinkle chopped onions, tomatoes, green chili, and coriander on top.
  4. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges and on top.
  5. Cook on medium flame until the base is golden brown and crisp.
  6. Flip and cook for another minute if needed. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.

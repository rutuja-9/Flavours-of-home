Ingredients

  • 4 taco shells
  • 1 cup boiled kidney beans (mashed)
  • Onion, tomato, lettuce
  • Grated cheese
  • Spices, chili flakes, taco seasoning

Instructions

  1. Prepare spicy bean filling. Chop veggies.
  2. Fill taco shells with beans, veggies, and cheese.
  3. Serve immediately while crispy.

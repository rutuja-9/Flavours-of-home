Cheesecake

Creamy, smooth, and indulgent — a classic baked cheesecake that melts in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups crushed digestive biscuits
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 3 eggs
  • Fruit topping or sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C (320°F).
  2. Mix crushed biscuits with butter. Press into a springform pan and chill.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add vanilla, sour cream, and eggs. Mix gently until smooth.
  5. Pour over crust and bake for 50–60 minutes.
  6. Cool completely and refrigerate for a few hours before serving.
  7. Add fruit topping if desired.

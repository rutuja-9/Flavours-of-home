Medu Vada

Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside â€“ deep-fried savory doughnuts made with urad dal, a South Indian favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tsp ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tbsp curry leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Water (as needed for grinding)

Instructions

  1. Soak urad dal for 4-5 hours and drain.
  2. Grind to a thick, fluffy batter with minimal water.
  3. Mix in ginger, green chilies, curry leaves, and salt.
  4. Wet your hands, take a portion of batter, shape into a doughnut with a hole in the center.
  5. Deep fry in hot oil on medium flame until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

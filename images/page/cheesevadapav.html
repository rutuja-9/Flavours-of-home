Cheese Vada Pav

A cheesy twist to the classic Mumbai Vada Pav – spicy potato filling, crisp fried coating, soft bun, and melted cheese!

Ingredients

  • 4 pav buns
  • 4 cheese slices or grated cheese
  • Oil for frying
  • Dry garlic chutney (optional)
  • For Vada Filling:
  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 chopped green chilies
  • Few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • For Batter:
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to make thick batter

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin, green chilies, curry leaves, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté.
  2. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, salt, and coriander. Mix well and let it cool.
  3. Make small balls of this mixture.
  4. In a bowl, prepare a thick batter using besan, turmeric, salt, and water.
  5. Dip the potato balls in batter and deep fry till golden brown.
  6. Slit pav buns, place cheese slice or sprinkle grated cheese inside, then place a hot vada.
  7. Add garlic chutney or green chutney if desired.
  8. Toast the pav slightly on a pan (optional) and serve hot.

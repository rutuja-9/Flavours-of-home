Kheer

A creamy and rich Indian rice pudding flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup basmati rice
  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, cashews)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil milk and add soaked rice. Cook on low flame till rice softens and milk thickens.
  3. Add sugar, cardamom, and saffron. Mix well.
  4. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve hot or chilled.

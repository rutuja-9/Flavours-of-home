Veg Biryani

Aromatic and flavorful rice dish made with vegetables, spices, and herbs — a festive and hearty lunch option.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1.5 cups mixed vegetables
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric & red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp curd
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook rice separately with whole spices and set aside.
  2. Heat oil, sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add vegetables, spices, curd, and cook well.
  4. Layer rice over the cooked veggies. Cover and simmer for 10 mins.
  5. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Chocolate Ice Cream

A rich and creamy homemade chocolate ice cream perfect for summer treats!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate (melted)
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Instructions

  1. Whisk cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Fold in condensed milk, cocoa, vanilla, and melted chocolate.
  3. Pour into a container and freeze for 6-8 hours.
  4. Scoop and serve chilled.

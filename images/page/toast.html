Bread Pakora

A popular North Indian snack made by dipping bread slices in a spicy gram flour batter and deep-frying them.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Water (as needed to make a batter)
  • Oil for frying
  • Chutney for serving

Instructions

  1. Mix besan, turmeric, red chili powder, ajwain, ginger-garlic paste, and salt in a bowl. Gradually add water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  3. Take a slice of bread, dip it into the batter to coat it completely, and then fry it in hot oil until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Drain excess oil on a paper towel and serve hot with chutney.

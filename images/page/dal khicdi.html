Dal Khichadi

A comfort food made with rice and moong dal, lightly spiced and ideal for a light, nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 inch ginger (grated)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2.5 cups water

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and dal. Heat ghee, add cumin and ginger.
  2. Add rice, dal, turmeric, salt, and water.
  3. Pressure cook for 3-4 whistles.
  4. Serve hot with ghee, papad, or pickle.

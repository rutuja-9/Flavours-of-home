Garlic Naan

Soft, fluffy, and buttery Indian naan flavored with garlic â€” a restaurant-style favorite made at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • Water to knead
  • Chopped garlic and butter

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients to form a soft dough. Rest for 1 hour.
  2. Divide into balls. Roll into oval shape, sprinkle garlic.
  3. Cook on hot tawa until golden, flip and cook other side.
  4. Apply butter and serve hot.

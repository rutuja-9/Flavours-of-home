Dhokla

A soft, fluffy, and savory steamed cake made from rice and chickpea flour, popular in Gujarat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • For tempering: 1 tbsp oil, 1/2 tsp mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies
  • Water (for batter consistency)

Instructions

  1. Mix chickpea flour, yogurt, turmeric, baking soda, ginger paste, and salt with enough water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Grease a steaming tray and pour the batter into it.
  3. Steam the dhokla for 15-20 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  4. For tempering, heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies, and fry for a few seconds.
  5. Pour the tempering over the steamed dhokla. Cut into pieces and serve hot with chutney.

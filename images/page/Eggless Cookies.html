Cookies

Crispy on the edges and chewy in the center — perfect homemade cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup choco chips or nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream together butter and sugars.
  3. Add vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix into wet ingredients.
  5. Add choco chips or nuts.
  6. Scoop dough onto a baking tray lined with parchment.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until edges are golden.

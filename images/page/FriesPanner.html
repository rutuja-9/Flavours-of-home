Pan Fried Paneer

Spicy and crispy paneer cubes shallow-fried to perfection — quick, tasty, and protein-rich!

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, lemon juice, and a bit of oil.
  2. Coat the paneer cubes in this mixture and let it marinate for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a non-stick pan and place the paneer cubes in a single layer.
  4. Fry on medium heat until golden and crispy on all sides.
  5. Sprinkle chaat masala and serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.

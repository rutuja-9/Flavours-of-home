Onion Pakora

Crunchy and flavorful deep-fried onion fritters, perfect with a cup of tea.

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix thinly sliced onions with besan, red chili powder, turmeric, ajwain, and salt.
  2. Gradually add water to form a thick batter, ensuring the mixture coats the onions well.
  3. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  4. Drop small portions of the batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Drain excess oil on paper towels and serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

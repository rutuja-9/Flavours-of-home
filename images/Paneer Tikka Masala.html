Paneer Tikka Masala

Grilled paneer cubes in a spicy, tangy curry – a restaurant-style favorite!

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 tsp tikka masala or garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt & chili powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate paneer in curd, spices, and grill or roast it.
  2. In a pan, heat oil, sauté onion, then add tomato puree and spices.
  3. Add grilled paneer and cook till well blended.
  4. Serve with paratha or steamed rice.

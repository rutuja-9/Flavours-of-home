Thepla

A spiced Gujarati flatbread made with whole wheat flour and fenugreek leaves.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup chopped fresh fenugreek (methi) leaves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp curd (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to knead
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix wheat flour, fenugreek leaves, spices, curd, and salt.
  2. Add water little by little to make a soft dough. Rest for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Divide into small balls and roll each into a thin round.
  4. Heat a tawa and cook each thepla on both sides using a little oil.
  5. Serve hot with pickle or curd.

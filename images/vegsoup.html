Veg Soup

A healthy and hearty mix of vegetables simmered with spices for a delicious and comforting soup.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1/4 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 tbsp cornflour mixed with water (for thickening)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups water or vegetable stock
  • Spring onion greens for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan, sauté garlic and onions until translucent.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and sauté for 3–4 minutes.
  3. Pour water or vegetable stock and bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer for 10–12 minutes until veggies are cooked.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and cornflour slurry. Cook until slightly thickened.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

Crispy Corn

Crispy and crunchy corn kernels, coated with a flavorful batter and deep-fried â€“ a perfect snack for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (cornstarch)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup water (for batter)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine corn kernels, cornflour, all-purpose flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Add water little by little and mix until the corn kernels are coated with the batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the corn in small batches until they turn crispy and golden brown.
  4. Remove the fried corn from the oil and place them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with your favorite chutney or dip.

Veg Grilled Sandwich

A hearty and tasty sandwich filled with spiced veggies and grilled to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 8 bread slices
  • 1 boiled potato, sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Green chutney (mint-coriander)
  • Butter
  • Chaat masala (optional)
  • Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Spread butter and chutney on bread slices.
  2. Layer potato, cucumber, and tomato slices. Sprinkle chaat masala and cheese if using.
  3. Cover with another slice and grill in a sandwich maker or tawa until golden.
  4. Cut diagonally and serve hot with ketchup or chutney.

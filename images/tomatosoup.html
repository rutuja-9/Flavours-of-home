Tomato Soup

A warm, comforting, and creamy tomato soup – perfect for cozy evenings and healthy appetites!

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 small carrot (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water or vegetable stock
  • Cream and croutons for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan, sauté onions, garlic, and carrot for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add some water or stock and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cool slightly and blend the mixture until smooth.
  5. Strain the soup using a sieve for a smooth texture.
  6. Reheat, add sugar, salt, and pepper. Adjust consistency with more water if needed.
  7. Serve hot with a swirl of cream and crunchy croutons on top.

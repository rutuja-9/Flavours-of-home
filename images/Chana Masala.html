Chana Masala

A hearty chickpea curry infused with bold Indian spices — filling and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chana masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric & red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions in oil, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook till oil leaves the sides.
  3. Add boiled chickpeas and simmer for 10 mins.
  4. Garnish with coriander and serve with roti or rice.

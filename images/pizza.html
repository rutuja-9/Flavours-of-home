Pizza

A classic Italian favorite made with a crispy crust, tangy sauce, cheese, and veggie toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup onion, capsicum, olives (sliced)
  • Oregano and chili flakes
  • Butter or olive oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Spread pizza sauce over the base, add toppings and cheese.
  3. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Slice and serve hot.

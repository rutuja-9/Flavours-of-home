Hakka Noodles

Quick stir-fried noodles with colorful veggies and bold Indo-Chinese flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled noodles
  • Sliced onion, capsicum, carrot, cabbage
  • Soy sauce, vinegar, green chili sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, stir-fry veggies on high flame.
  2. Add noodles and sauces.
  3. Toss everything together. Serve hot with Manchurian.

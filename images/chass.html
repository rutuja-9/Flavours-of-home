Chass (Buttermilk)

A light, cooling, and digestive Indian drink made with yogurt, spices, and herbs – perfect for hot days!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain curd (yogurt)
  • 2 cups chilled water
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 green chili (optional, finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Few mint leaves (optional)
  • A pinch of black salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Whisk the curd until smooth.
  2. Add chilled water and whisk again until well blended and frothy.
  3. Add cumin powder, salt, black salt, green chili, and chopped herbs.
  4. Mix well and chill for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Serve cold, garnished with coriander or mint leaves.

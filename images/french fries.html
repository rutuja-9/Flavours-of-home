French Fries

Golden and crispy fries made from fresh potatoes â€” a classic and loved snack!

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Chaat masala (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin sticks. Soak in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain and pat dry. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  3. Fry in batches on medium heat until golden and crisp.
  4. Sprinkle salt or chaat masala and serve hot.

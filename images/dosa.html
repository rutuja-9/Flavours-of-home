Dosa

Thin, crispy South Indian crepes made from fermented rice and lentil batter â€“ perfect for breakfast or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for soaking and grinding)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking dosa

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal to a fluffy consistency and rice to a smooth batter.
  3. Mix both batters, add salt, and ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours.
  4. Heat a non-stick or cast-iron tawa. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it into a thin circle.
  5. Drizzle oil or ghee on the edges and cook until golden brown and crisp.
  6. Fold and serve hot with chutney and sambar.

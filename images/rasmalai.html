Rasmalai

Soft paneer balls soaked in saffron milk, a royal Bengali dessert!

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk (for paneer)
  • 1 liter milk (for rabri)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands
  • Nuts for garnish

Instructions

  1. Make paneer, knead, and shape into balls. Boil in sugar water.
  2. Boil milk, reduce, add sugar, cardamom, saffron.
  3. Squeeze rasgullas and soak in rabri.
  4. Cool and garnish with nuts.

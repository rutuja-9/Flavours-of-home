Soft and crispy round dumplings made from fermented batter, a delicious South Indian breakfast or snack option.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli/dosa batter (fermented)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 5-6 curry leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste (if needed)
  • Oil for greasing appe pan

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat a little oil and temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, and green chilies. Saut√© until soft.
  2. Add this mixture to the dosa/idli batter. Mix in coriander and adjust salt if needed.
  3. Heat an appe (paniyaram) pan and grease the cavities with oil.
  4. Pour batter into each cavity and cover with a lid.
  5. Cook on medium flame until golden at the bottom, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney or tomato chutney.

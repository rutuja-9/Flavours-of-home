Spring Roll

Crispy rolls filled with stir-fried veggies â€” a perfect snack or party starter!

Ingredients

  • Spring roll sheets
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan and stir-fry all the vegetables on high flame.
  2. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Let the mixture cool.
  3. Place filling in spring roll sheets, roll tightly, and seal the edges.
  4. Heat oil and deep fry the rolls until golden and crispy.
  5. Cut diagonally and serve with chili or garlic sauce.

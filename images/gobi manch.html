Gobi Manchurian

Crispy fried cauliflower florets tossed in spicy Indo-Chinese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1/4 cup cornflour + maida
  • Chili sauce, soy sauce, vinegar
  • Garlic, spring onion, capsicum
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil and coat gobi in batter. Deep fry till crispy.

  3. Add gobi and toss until well coated. Serve hot.

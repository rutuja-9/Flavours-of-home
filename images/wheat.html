Wheat Roti

A soft and nutritious Indian flatbread made with whole wheat flour â€” a staple in Indian meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Water as needed
  • Pinch of salt (optional)
  • 1 tsp oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, salt, and water. Knead into a soft dough.
  2. Rest for 20 minutes. Divide into equal balls.
  3. Roll each into a thin circle and cook on a hot tawa.
  4. Flip until both sides are golden and slightly puffed.
  5. Brush with ghee and serve warm.

Schezwan Noodles

Spicy stir-fried noodles infused with Schezwan sauce and vibrant vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked noodles
  • 2 tbsp Schezwan sauce
  • Capsicum, onion, carrot, cabbage
  • Soy sauce, vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add veggies and stir-fry on high flame.
  2. Add Schezwan sauce, noodles, and mix well.
  3. Garnish and serve spicy noodles hot.

Burger

A delicious snack with crispy veggie patty, fresh veggies, cheese, and sauces stacked between soft buns.

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 veggie patties
  • 2 cheese slices
  • Tomato, onion, lettuce slices
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard
  • Butter for toasting

Instructions

  1. Toast buns with butter. Shallow fry or bake patties.
  2. Apply sauces on buns, add patty, veggies, and cheese.
  3. Close the bun and serve hot with fries.

Sabudana Khichdi

A delicious and light Maharashtrian fasting recipe made with soaked sabudana, peanuts, and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sabudana (sago pearls)
  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled and diced)
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts (coarsely ground)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • Salt to taste (sendha namak if fasting)
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Rinse sabudana and soak in water for 5-6 hours or overnight. Drain well.
  2. Mix soaked sabudana with crushed peanuts and salt.
  3. Heat ghee/oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and green chilies.
  4. Add diced potatoes and saut√© for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the sabudana-peanut mixture and stir on low flame until sabudana becomes translucent.
  6. Turn off the flame. Garnish with coriander and lemon juice.
  7. Serve warm.

Kaju Curry

A rich and royal cashew-based curry that's creamy and full of flavor â€” a restaurant favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup kaju (cashews)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp cream
  • 1/2 tsp cumin & garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric & chili powder
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast cashews lightly in ghee and set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook onion till golden, add tomato puree and spices.
  3. Add cashews and cream, simmer for 5 mins.
  4. Serve hot with naan or rice.
